## What it is

BTR is the protocol's native ERC-20 token. It captures protocol value (fee share via staking, future governance weight) and aligns long-term holders with the protocol's success.

## Why it matters to users

- **Earn**: stake BTR to receive a share of protocol fees (Phase 2).
- **Vote**: vote on protocol parameters and treasury proposals once on-chain governance ships (Phase 2; currently advisory).
- **Discount**: future fee rebates for BTR holders on supported surfaces.

## Key facts

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Standard | ERC-20 |
| Max supply | 100,000,000 BTR (hard cap, enforced on-chain) |
| Emission | Halving schedule into stake pools (see [Emissions](./09.%20Emissions.md)) |
