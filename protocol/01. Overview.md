# Protocol Overview

> Shared protocol-level surfaces spanning ALM (Vaults) and DEX (AIMM Pools)

---

## 1. Scope

> For the cross-surface product matrix, see [Concepts → Overview](../concepts/overview.md).

The **Protocol layer** contains contracts and modules that are **shared across both the ALM (Vault) and DEX (AIMM Pool) product surfaces**. These primitives provide a single source of truth for access control, treasury policy, staking, emissions, governance, and cross-chain bridging - used uniformly by every product the protocol ships.

---

## 2. Why Protocol-Level?

Both **ALM Vaults** (concentrated-liquidity managers on top of external AMMs) and **DEX AIMM Pools** (native protocol pools) share identical organisational needs:

- A single canonical admin / keeper / treasury registry (`AccessControl`)
- A single treasury receiving every fee stream
- A single emission schedule funding any stake pool the DAO whitelists
- A single governance authority approving param changes on either surface
- A single bridge moving BTR and stake receipts between chains

Duplicating these per-surface would fragment ownership, treasury, and governance. Keeping them at the **protocol layer** ensures consistency and reduces audit surface.
