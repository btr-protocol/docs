# 9. Parking & Swap Policy (Base)

## Summary

| Exposure | Leverage | Swap | Park proceeds in |
|----------|----------|------|------------------|
| LONG | None (unlevered) | Atomic **or** LiFi Intent | ERC-4626 yield vault |
| LONG | Morpho flash | **Atomic only** | Morpho collateral (not yield vault) |
| SHORT | Morpho flash + Moonwell | **Atomic only** | Moonwell mToken supply (USDC collateral) |
| Cash (USDC idle) | — | — | Clearstar USDC Reactor |

**Rule:** Intents and Morpho flash are incompatible (escrow locks funds before flash can repay). Unlevered entry is two keeper steps when using intents: (1) swap/settle, (2) `enterUnlevered` or `parkIdle`.

## Default Base addresses

Canonical constants: `shared/evm/src/config/BaseParking.sol` and `@btr-protocol/sdk/eth` `base-parking.ts`.

SHORT borrowed exposure (e.g. cbBTC) is sold for USDC via atomic swap — it is **not** parked in a yield vault.

## On-chain entrypoints (`LevVault`)

| Function | When |
|----------|------|
| `parkIdle(token, amount)` | Keeper parks idle cash or proceeds |
| `enterUnlevered(usdcIn, routerData, minOut)` | Swap + yield vault (atomic calldata) |
| `exitUnlevered(shares, routerData, minOut)` | Redeem yield vault + swap to USDC |
| `openPosition` / `closePosition` | Morpho LONG leverage (atomic) |
| `openShortPosition` / `closeShortPosition` | Moonwell SHORT book (atomic) |

Shared swap stack: `@btr-supply/swap` + `back/services/swap` — same LiFi surface for ALM and Prime; keeper selects `kind: "atomic" | "intent"` via `route(levered)` in SDK.

## ALM vs Prime

- **ALM** (`CLAdapter`): intent rebalance when inventory exists and route is unlevered; atomic for keeper `/build`.
- **Prime** (`LevVault`): leveraged paths atomic-only; unlevered long may use intents off-chain then `enterUnlevered` on-chain.
