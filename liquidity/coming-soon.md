# Liquidity - Coming Soon

Direct LP provisioning into BTR's own AIMM (Automated Intelligent Market Maker) pools will be available in **Phase 2**, alongside the BTR DEX launch.

For now, the recommended way to earn yield is to deposit into a [Vault](../vaults/01.%20Overview.md). Vaults already deploy capital into concentrated-liquidity strategies on supported venues.

See the roadmap (announcement forthcoming) for the latest timing.
