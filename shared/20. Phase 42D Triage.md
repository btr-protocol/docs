## 2. Panel dissent log

- **R2-A2-1 (executeSwap isPool → isOfficialPool):** Architect IMPLEMENT, Security IMPLEMENT, Conventions DISCARD. Conventions argued: SDK integrators may have legitimate use cases routing through factory-created-but-unofficial pools (e.g. private liquidity venues). Architect+Security counter: pool registration is already owner-gated, and asymmetric quote-vs-exec gating violates principle of least surprise. **Consensus IMPLEMENT** -if integrators want to route through unofficial pools they can call the pool directly (Router is not the only entrypoint). Conventions noted for record.

No other dissents -all other items reached unanimous 3/3 consensus.
