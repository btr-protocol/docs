### 2.2 F-A1-R16-3 (CRITICAL) -StakedToken mint/burn surface

  - `Pool.donate:72-93` does NOT touch `lpBalances` -only rebases `liquidityIndex`. Staked LPs benefit from donate via index appreciation; on `unstakeLP` they restore the same share count which is now worth more. Conservation ✓.

Functionally fine but semantically asymmetric. LP tokens are pool ERC20 shares, never NATIVE in practice. INFO only -see F-A1-R17-1 below.
