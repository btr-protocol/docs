## 10. Storage namespacing (ERC-7201)

Currently 6 distinct slots @ `LibConstants.sol:7–18`:
- `CORE_STORAGE_LOC` (Pool main)
- `STAKING_STORAGE_LOC`
- `DISTRIBUTOR_STORAGE_LOC`
- `ORACLE_STORAGE_LOC`
- `RESCUE_STORAGE_LOC`
- `FLOW_GUARD_STORAGE_LOC`

**Verdict:** **Keep separate.** Each module owns its own slot for upgrade-safety (ERC-7201 entire purpose). Collapsing to 1 namespace would force `IPool.PoolStorage` to expand, increasing struct churn during module updates and risking storage-layout corruption. **Δ = 0.**
