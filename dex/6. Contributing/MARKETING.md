## 1. Strategic Positioning

### Core Positioning Statement

**BTR is a trust-first, performance-based DEX built for sophisticated LPs and traders who value audited security, transparent risk frameworks, and verifiable performance over mercenary yield chasing.**

| Attribute | BTR Approach | Vampire/Mercenary Alternative |
|-----------|--------------|-------------------------------|
| **Trust** | Audits, monitoring dashboards, reproducible methodology | Incentive-heavy, short-term TVL pumping |
| **Performance** | Yield + execution quality with published benchmarks | APY claims without context |
| **Growth** | Organic through credibility artifacts | Paid KOLs, aggressive campaigns |
| **Compounding** | Slower but sustainable | Faster but higher churn |

### Credibility Artifacts (First-Class Marketing Outputs)

**Trust is our moat. These artifacts are as important as feature announcements:**

| Artifact | Owner | Update Frequency | Status |
|----------|-------|------------------|--------|

- Referenced in marketing (link, don't just claim)

## 2. Brand Voice & Messaging

### Voice Characteristics

| Trait | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Expert but accessible** | DeFi made simple, never dumbed down | "Capital efficiency for everyone: lower slippage through inventory-aware pricing" |
| **Transparent** | Honest about risks, assumptions, limitations | "APY varies with volatility; historical 7-day return: X%, with Y% drawdown during Z event" |
| **Innovative but grounded** | Forward-thinking with proven foundations | "Building on Avellaneda-Stoikov with ALM-based coverage management" |
| **Community-first** | User-focused, not token-price focused | "Built by traders, for traders, LPs earn from cooperative arbitrage" |

### Tiered Messaging Stack

**Different funnel depths require different proof levels:**

#### Top-of-Funnel (Awareness)
*Simple outcomes, no jargon*

| Message | Supporting Proof |
|---------|------------------|
| Better execution than traditional AMMs | Link to methodology |
| Simpler LPing: deposit one token, not pairs | Demo video |
| Transparent risk with coverage ratios | Dashboard screenshot |

#### Mid-Funnel (Consideration)
*Mechanisms and differentiation*

| Message | Supporting Proof |
|---------|------------------|
| Cooperative arbitrage returns LVR to LPs | Blog post with math |
| Inventory-aware pricing reduces adverse selection | Research paper comparison |
| Dual-window TWAP resists manipulation | Technical diagram + code reference |

#### Bottom-Funnel (Conversion)
*Evidence and verifiable claims*

| Message | Supporting Proof |
|---------|------------------|
| Audited by [firm] | Audit report PDF |
| X months live with Y TVL and Z exploits | Transparency report |
| Historical LP returns vs Uniswap v3 | Backtesting disclosure |

### Proof Standards

**Publishing comparative claims safely:**

```markdown
## Backtesting Disclosure Template

**Period**: [start_date] to [end_date]
**Markets**: [list of pools/tokens]
**Fee Assumption**: [basis points, any changes]
**Slippage Assumption**: [bps per trade]
**Rebalancing Frequency**: [description]
**Survivorship Bias**: [addressed/not applicable; if pools removed, explain why]
**Limitations**: [what this does NOT capture: MEV, validator tips, etc.]

**Peer Comparison Methodology**:
- Data source: [Dune/Nansen/DexScreener]
- Time window: [identical period for all compared protocols]
- Pool selection: [criteria: similar TVL range, same token pairs]
- Normalization: [how fees/IL are calculated for fair comparison]

**Risk-Adjusted Reporting**:
- Volatility bands: [5th/25th/50th/75th/95th percentile returns]
- Max drawdown: [date and magnitude]
- Stress scenarios: [what happened during major market events]
```

### Messaging Do's and Don'ts

| Do | Don't |
|-----|-------|
| "Historical x% APY over Y period" | "You will earn x%" |
| "Advanced risk management with coverage ratios" | "Risk-free" or "Safe yield" |
| "Competitive fees/slippage vs [protocol]" with methodology link | "Lowest fees/slippage" without proof |
| "Passive income with active arbitrage rebates" | "Guaranteed returns" |
| "Impermanent loss may occur; fees often offset" (neutral framing) | "No impermanent loss" |
| "Sybil-resistant airdrop with published criteria" | "Free money incoming" |

## 4. Social Media Strategy

### Platform-Specific Tactics

**X (Twitter):**
- 1-3 tweets daily Monday→Friday
- Mix: 40% education, 30% announcements, 20% engagement, 10% updates
- Favor single posts with linked articles over threads for complex topics
- Engage with replies within 4 hours
- Hashtags: max 4 per post, none on short posts/responses
- Tags: #DeFi #AMM #Liquidity #Yield #Stablecoins #RWA

**Telegram:**
- **Major announcements**: Features, audits, bounties, partnerships, TVL milestones
- **Major discussions**: Governance, pool deployment, asset listing, co-op arbitrage, tokenomics
- **Secondary channels**: NSFW/troll-box (clearly labeled)
- **Weekly AMA** + community check-in
- **Support SLA**: Automated response <5min, manual <12 hours
- **Price/speculative discussion**: Discouraged, redirect if necessary
- **Moderation**: Transparent rules, labeled admins, predictable enforcement (see §7)

**LinkedIn (Enhanced):**
- **Primary goal**: TradFi/institutional LP acquisition
- **Content flow**: Funnel to Telegram + docs + demo requests
- **Cross-posting**: Technical deep-dives from blog, research reports
- **Partnerships**: Joint posts with integrations
- **Thought leadership**: Original content on AMM design, risk management

## 6. Growth Tactics

### Partnership Framework

**Philosophy**: We don't pay for integrations. Aggregators, wallets, and optimizers will integrate BTR because better liquidity = better execution for their users. Our product is the incentive.

| Partner Type | Value Proposition | Outreach |
|--------------|------------------|----------|
| **DEX Aggregators** | Better routing = happier users | Technical documentation, integration support |
| **Yield Aggregators** | Access to AIMM's yield for their users | Same as above |
| **Wallets** | Default integration = better UX | Same as above |
| **Influencers** | Content creation | Affiliate links (performance-based) |
| **Optimizers** | Yield routing to AIMM pools | Technical integration only |

**Integration support provided:**
- Comprehensive API documentation
- Testnet environment for testing
- Direct engineering support
- Co-marketing upon completion

### Referral Program

**Simple, fair, single-tier:**

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Rate** | 10% of protocol trading fees from referred users |
| **Tiers** | None, flat rate for everyone |
| **Conditions** | None, no volume requirements, no retention gates |
| **Payout** | Automatic, per claim cycle |

**Philosophy**: Keep it minimal. No gamification, no tier jumping, no condition chasing. A referrer brings users, BTR pays 10% of fees those users generate. Simple is sustainable.

### Airdrop Strategy

1. **Snapshot** at key milestones (audit, mainnet, TVL targets)
2. **Qualification tiers**:
   - Staked LPs: weighted by duration × amount
   - sBTR owners: weighted by duration × amount
   - Traders: weighted by volume × fee generation (**industry first for spot DEX**)
3. **Sybil resistance**:
   - Minimum activity thresholds
   - Cluster analysis for wallet linkage
   - Proof of humanity / Gitcoin Passport consideration
4. **Vesting**: See tokenomics docs
5. **Claim**: See tokenomics docs
6. **Transparency**: Publish full methodology + snapshot data (except PII)

## 7. Community Safety & Moderation

### Telegram Moderation Standards

**Transparent but strict, with AI automation:**

| Policy | Rule | Enforcement |
|--------|------|-------------|
| **Scam links** | Zero tolerance | Immediate ban (AI auto-detect), log in public channel |
| **Spam** | >3 similar messages/day | Warning, then mute (AI auto-detect) |
| **Price speculation** | Redirect to price-talk channel | Move message, no ban (AI or manual) |
| **FUD without evidence** | Require sources | Tag, otherwise hide (AI flag for review) |
| **Admin impersonation** | Zero tolerance | Immediate ban (AI auto-detect) |

**AI/IA Automation:**
- **Censorship**: Auto-hide scam links, spam patterns, malicious content
- **Banning**: Auto-ban confirmed scammers, spammers, impersonators
- **Role assignment**: Auto-assign verified roles based on on-chain activity (LP, trader, staker)
- **Sentiment monitoring**: Flag negative sentiment spikes for human review

**Procedural transparency:**
- Pinned rules message (updated per version)
- Admin labels in username (e.g., "Admin - John", "AI Moderator")
- Ban log (public, anonymized for privacy)
- Appeal process (email + review within 48h)
- AI actions logged and auditable

### Crisis Communications

**Template + Protocol-Specific Runbook:**

```markdown

2. [Status of funds: safe / impacted / under investigation]

## Next Steps
1. [Post-mortem timeline]
2. [Fund recovery actions, if applicable]
3. [Protocol resumption plan]
```

**Protocol-Specific Runbooks (internal):**

|----------|---------|---------|-------|

| **Bridge issue** | Failed message spike | Pause bridging, queue review | Status updates |
| **Liquidity stress** | Withdrawal surge | Monitor coverage, pause if needed | Transparent metrics |
| **Smart contract issue** | Suspicious activity | Emergency pause, investigation | Incident template above |
