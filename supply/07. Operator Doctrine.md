# 7. Operator Doctrine

> Who can do what, when, and how the contracts enforce the separation.

---

## 1. Roles

| Role | Source | Privileges |
|------|--------|------------|
| **Owner** | `AccessControl.owner()` | Full control of governance -fees, knobs, kill switch, adapter add/remove, salvage, factory/treasury/swapper queueing. |
| **Keeper** | `AccessControl.isKeeper[a]` | Defensive ratchets, kill (rate-limited + capped), allocate/collectFrom, rebalance, harvestPerformance. |
| **Adapter** | `AccessControl.isAdapter[a]` | Charges keeper-kill against the cluster cap (registered by Factory only). |
| **Holder** | `Dex.isHolder[a]` | Calls `pull` / `withdraw` on a Dex adapter. Vaults are holders; EOAs / multisigs may also be holders. |
| **Operator** (per-controller) | `Vault.isOperator[ctrl][a]` | Standing approval to call `redeem` / `requestRedeem` / `claimRedeem` for a specific controller (ERC-7540). |

## 3. Owner-or-Keeper Actions

`Vault`:

- `setConfig("depositCap", v)` -keeper may only ratchet **down** and never below `totalAssets()` (`Vault.sol:411`).
- `allocate` / `collectFrom` (`:502`, `:512`).
- `harvestPerformance` (`:442`).

`Dex`:

- `setConfig("maxSlipBps", v)` -keeper may only ratchet **down** (`Dex.sol:186`).
- `setConfig("minIntervalSecs", v)` -keeper may only ratchet **up**, by at most `KEEPER_INTERVAL_DELTA = 1 day` per call (`:191`).
- `kill()` -keeper rate-limited (`KILL_RATE_LIMIT = 1 hour`) + cluster cap (`AC.chargeKeeperKill`, daily cap = 2).
- `rebalance` (`:304`).

The pattern: **keepers can only move risk knobs in the safer direction**. The owner is the only authority that can loosen them -and freeze-trigger params (`deviationBps`, `heartbeatDriftBps`) are timelocked even for the owner.

## 4. Permissionless Actions

| Action | Site | Why |
|--------|------|-----|
| `Vault.syncAdapter(a)` | `Vault.sol:520` | Restore health post-outage without waiting for owner. |
| `Vault.processWithdrawals(controllers[])` | `:309` | Anyone can settle the cohort; haircut is uniform. |
| `Vault.claimRedeem(controller, receiver)` | `:364` | Caller must be controller or its operator. |
| `Swapper.settleIntent(intentId)` | `Swapper.sol:218` | Anyone can sweep filled tokenOut to the caller. |
| `Factory.deployVault(asset, ...)` | `Factory.sol:37` | Inert clone until owner adds adapters. |

## 5. Timelocks Summary

| Subject | Constant | Site | Bypass |
|---------|----------|------|--------|
| Adapter freeze-trigger params | `FREEZE_TIMELOCK = 1 day` | `Dex.sol:50` | None. Even owner waits. |
| Adapter unkill | `FREEZE_TIMELOCK = 1 day` | `Dex.sol:50` | None. Defangs replay-toggle by compromised owner. |
| Treasury / Swapper rotation | `ROTATION_TIMELOCK = 7 days` | `AccessControl.sol:15` | First bind only via `bootstrap*`. |
| Factory rotation | `FACTORY_TIMELOCK = 14 days` | `AccessControl.sol:16` | First bind only via `bootstrapFactory`. |
| Swapper router/settler rotation | `ROUTER_TIMELOCK = 7 days` | `Swapper.sol:20` | First bind only via `bootstrapLifi` / `bootstrapSettler`. |
| Price feed swap (existing token) | `FEED_TIMELOCK = 6 hours` | `PriceProvider.sol:23` | First-time `setFeed` instant. |
| Intent grace before forceRefund | `INTENT_GRACE = 6 hours` | `Swapper.sol:21` | None. |

## 6. Defensive Ratchets (Keeper Direction)

| Knob | Permitted keeper direction |
|------|----------------------------|
| `Vault.depositCap` | ↓ only, ≥ `totalAssets()` |
| `Dex.maxSlipBps` | ↓ only |
| `Dex.minIntervalSecs` | ↑ only, ≤ current + `KEEPER_INTERVAL_DELTA` |

The principle: a keeper can always make the system **more** defensive. Loosening always requires the owner.

## 7. Daily Kill Cap

`AccessControl.chargeKeeperKill()` (`AccessControl.sol:77`):

- Adapter-callable only (`isAdapter[msg.sender]` required).
- UTC-day epoch; 2 keeper kills per UTC day across the **entire cluster**.
- Owner-initiated kills bypass this cap (call adapter `kill` directly without going through AC).

Without this cap, a single keeper × 8 adapters × 1/h could freeze a vault for 8 hours. The cap forces a real human (owner) into the loop on the second incident of the day.

## 8. Two-Step Ownership

`AccessControl` overrides Solady's 1-step `transferOwnership` and `renounceOwnership` to revert (`AccessControl.sol:60–61`). Handover requires:

1. Current owner → `requestOwnershipHandover(newOwner)` (Solady, time-windowed).
2. New owner → `completeOwnershipHandover(pendingOwner)` (Solady, with zero-target rejection -`AccessControl.sol:64`).

The zero-target rejection closes the back-door renounce vector.

## 9. Operator Playbook (Routine)

| Frequency | Action | Authority |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Continuous | Monitor `_healthy()` per vault, `frozen()` per adapter | -|
| On vol-regime change | `Dex.rebalance(tl, tu, routes)` | Owner / Keeper |
| Daily–weekly | `Vault.allocate` / `collectFrom` to balance idle vs deployed | Owner / Keeper |
| Weekly–monthly | `Vault.harvestPerformance` | Owner / Keeper |
| On request batch ready | `Vault.processWithdrawals(sorted_ctrls)` | Anyone |
| On open intent fill | `Swapper.settleIntent(id)` | Anyone |
| On adapter compromise | `AC.deregisterAdapter(a)` → `Vault.removeAdapter(a)` | Owner |
| On cap breach | `Vault.setConfig("depositCap", ↓)` | Owner / Keeper |
| On feed migration | `PriceProvider.queueFeed → executeFeed` (after 6h) | Owner |
